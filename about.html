Devin Krizwold

Born in San Francisco, I grew up surrounded by pictures and sounds of every flavor. I became infatuated with Looney Tunes and The Simpsons at a young age and like many, made imitating and being inspired by these voices a regular pastime. I've since renewed my passion for voices through studying voiceover at the Berkeley Rep School of Theatre under the exceptionally talanted Sally Clawson.
