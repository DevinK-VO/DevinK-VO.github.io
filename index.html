Me, My Voice, and I

I studied voiceover at the Berkeley Repertory Theatre School under the incredibly talented Sally Clawson, where I developed a voice that has been described as confident, smooth, resonant and versatile. You can find more demos on the Demos page. I work from a professional studio in Oakland, CA and can accomodate turn-around as quickly as 24hrs.

I would love to hear from you! Whether an opportunity to breathe life into your project or a shoutout from a fellow artist, drop me a line!
